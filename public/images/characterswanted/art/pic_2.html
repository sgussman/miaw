INterview with Laura Petree

Katharine Schwab with the Stanford Arts Review gets her freek on with Laura Petree. Want to learn more about the Freeks? Here's a taste:

﻿

"Free independent performance is the Freeks. Ideally, I want it to be that anyone who is creating free independent performance can use the name ‘the Freeks’ without contacting me or contacting anyone in the Freeks. I want it to just be this thing that happens and then you’re a Freek once you’ve done it.”

The FREEKS are proud to present, Almost, Maine, an immersive theater experience about nine couples dealing with the joys, difficulties, and bitter-sweetness of love.

 

Almost, Maine will be performed in the backyard of Haus Mitt, 620 Mayfield Avenue, from 8-10 pm, on April 3rd, 4th, and 5th.
